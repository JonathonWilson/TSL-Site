  1. No Combat Logging: You may NOT log out within 5 minutes of PvP to avoid a fight. This includes being shot at, being hit/shot, poxxed, or being within the vicinity of PvP.
  2. No Respawning After Knockout: You may NOT restart/respawn once being knocked out by a player. Just take the loss.
  3. Game Crashes in PvP: If your game crashes in the middle of PvP, you must clip the last 60 seconds and be prepared to show this when asked.
  4. No Safezone Avoidance: You may not run into a safezone to avoid PvP.

  1. Base Damage Period: Base Damage is on between Friday 1:00pm EST and Sunday 10:00pm EST. This doesn't stop you from hopping over a wall or cracking someone's code lock during the week when base damage is off.
  2. Base Claiming and Stealing: You may not claim/steal a base with a flagpole set up. You may ONLY steal or claim a base with no flagpole, and if you change all entrance locks leading to the heart of the base. If you're solo and do not want to lose your base, build a flag pole. DayZ flags can be collected from the trader.
  3. Disconnecting in Bases: Do not disconnect inside other people's bases under any circumstances.
  4. Respect for Base Items: Do not despawn/ruin/dismantle things unnecessarily. Do not deconstruct flag poles.
  5. No Unauthorized Building Modifications: You must NOT build into or in other people's bases.
  6. Boosting Raid Using Tents or Players: You may boost raid using tents or other players. Tents used for boosting must be packable and must not clip into walls/fences/etc.
  7. Exploitative Tactics Ban: You must NOT garden plot/fireplace/or swim to gain access to someone's base. Stay away from these exploits or you will be banned.
  8. Reclaiming Stolen Bases: If you log into a base that was previously yours but has been stolen, you are not allowed to raid it or log back out. If you log in to your now stolen base, you have three options:
  9. a. Suicide your character
  10. b. Jump out or dismantle out (only enough walls that lead to your exit)
  11. c. Contact the people who stole your base and make an arrangement for a peaceful exit.
  12. Risks of Stealing Bases: If you steal a base and the previous owners load in and kill you, that's the risk you take with stealing a base. They still must abide by all other above rules.
  13. Stolen Base on Raid Weekend: If you load in during a raid weekend, and your base is stolen, your ONLY option is to suicide your character.

  1. Military Area Building Restrictions: Do NOT build in the designated military areas listed below (within perimeter limits): M B Kamensk, M B Tisy, M B VMC, M B Zeleno, NWAF, Krasno Airfield, and any military checkpoints.
  2. Coastal Building Restrictions: Do not build in any coastal town or directly on the coast. This includes the whole of Berezino. This excludes the following (i.e. you can base in the towns mentioned below): Prison Island, Vysotovo, Novoselki, Dubovo, and Skalisty Island.
  3. Base Accessibility: You may not build an un-raidable base. If you have one entrance, that entrance must be a gate. If you're on a ladder, that ladder must be climbable. You may NOT use tents/watchtower logs to block entrances. You may NOT use logs or tents to block ladders. Ladders must be climbable. You may NOT block roads with fences/gates, etc.
  4. Claiming Buildings: You may only claim one large building for your faction (this includes anything that is connected to that building) and an unlimited number of small buildings. A large building may be a single apartment block, a double apartment block, a large warehouse, or connecting apartments. A small building may be a single house, garage, or shed.
  5. SkyBases Prohibition: No SkyBases.
  6. Flagpole Limit and Alliance Restrictions: You may build a maximum of 2 flagpoles to cover your base. You may NOT ally with a team and wall around multiple buildings via one large courtyard. They must be independent bases.
  7. Flag Usage and Faction Responsibilities: Flags may not be lowered for the intention of hiding them during raid weekend. A faction may only lower their own flag to move base or move the location of a flagpole. If you are in a faction, you must pick up your flag before raid weekend. Failure to do so risks faction disband or BMD fine.
  8. Storage Items Regulations: All storage items must not abuse the "build mod" and must be able to be packed and stolen. If you have to boost or build to unpack a tent, this placement is considered NOT allowed. You will be forced to destroy it and pay the raiders for the tent they couldn't steal.
  9. Watchtower Height Limit: Watchtowers can only be stacked two high/6 floors.
