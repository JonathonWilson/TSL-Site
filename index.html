TSL

TSL was set up by a group of players that were tired of looking for servers that had that vanilla feel with quality of life updates and admins that weren't obsessive and overly involved. The rules for The Savage Lands are simple and easy to follow, mainly because there aren't many and we still want that Dayz feel.
